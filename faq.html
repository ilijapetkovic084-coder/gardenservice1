FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything people usually ask before booking Muskashears in. Can't find it here — just call or text.

What areas do you cover?

Birmingham, Solihull, Chelmsley Wood, Castle Bromwich, Marston Green, Fordbridge and the surrounding B36, B37 and B46 postcodes. If you're just outside that area, get in touch anyway — happy to travel further for the right job.

How do I get a quote, and how much does it cost?

Quotes are free and no-obligation. Call or text a few details and, ideally, a couple of photos of the garden, and you'll usually get a straight answer back the same day. Bigger jobs are often easier to price after a quick look in person.

Are you insured?

Yes — fully insured with public liability cover on every job, and registered as a waste carrier for anything that needs taking away. Happy to show proof of either on request.

Do you take the garden waste away?

Yes. Grass cuttings, hedge trimmings, weeds and general green waste are cleared and taken away as part of the job under a registered waste carrier's licence — nothing left behind in bags on the drive.

My garden is really overgrown — can you still help?

That's genuinely a specialty. Gardens that haven't been touched in months or years get brought back under control regularly — no judgement, and no garden considered too far gone to sort out.

How often should I book a maintenance visit?

Whatever suits the garden and your budget — weekly, fortnightly or monthly maintenance plans are all available, alongside one-off tidy-ups. Worth a chat about how fast your particular garden grows before settling on a schedule.

Do I need to be home when you visit?

Not usually, as long as there's clear access to the garden — a side gate is often enough. Access arrangements are always agreed before the first visit, and you're welcome to be there if you'd prefer.

What happens if it's raining on the day?

Light rain doesn't usually stop work. If conditions are too wet or unsafe to do a proper job, the visit is rescheduled for the earliest opportunity at no extra cost.

Do you bring your own tools and equipment?

Yes — mowers, strimmers, hedge trimmers and everything else needed comes as standard. There's nothing you need to provide.

How do I book you in?

Simplest way is to call or text 07756 479238, or send a message on WhatsApp. A time and price get sorted out directly from there.
