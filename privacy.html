Privacy Policy

A short, plain-language explanation of what happens with your information if you get in touch.

Last updated: July 2026

Who this covers

This policy covers this website and any enquiry made to Muskashears Garden Service by phone, text or WhatsApp. Muskashears Garden Service is an independent gardening business based in the Birmingham area, run by Mark.

What information is collected

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts or advertising trackers, and it does not collect any information automatically when you browse it.

The only information collected is what you choose to share when you get in touch — typically your name, phone number, rough location, and details of the job you'd like done. This is normally given directly over a phone call, text message or WhatsApp message, not through this website.

How it's used

Information you share is used only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Arrange and carry out the gardening work you've booked
  • Keep basic records of regular customers for scheduling repeat visits

Sharing your information

Your information is not sold, rented or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. It's only ever disclosed to others where required by law — for example, waste transfer records connected to the registered waste carrier's licence.

How long it's kept

Contact details are kept only for as long as they're useful — generally for the duration of a working relationship with a customer, or until you ask for them to be removed.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you can ask what information is held about you, ask for it to be corrected if it's wrong, or ask for it to be deleted. To do any of these, just get in touch using the details below.

Links to other sites

This site links out to Google Maps and WhatsApp. Once you follow one of those links, you're covered by that service's own privacy policy rather than this one.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect how the business operates. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last changed.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information can be sent straight to Mark: 07756 479238.
